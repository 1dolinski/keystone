About

Keystone specializes in residential and commercial irrigation systems and has been servicing the Niagara Region for over 10 years. We are proudly owner operated and pride ourselves on premium service and high quality workmanship. We aim to provide customers with effective irrigation systems designed to efficiently water and to promote the healthy growth of all your lawn, gardens, hanging baskets and flower pots.

We strive to maintain excellence in customer service and professionalism. Our reputation for high ethical standards, honesty and integrity in service and attitude remains a # 1 priority.
